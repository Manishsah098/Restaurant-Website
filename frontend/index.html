Project Specification

The Restaurant Management System (RMS)
Running a restaurant is more than just cooking great food. It involves taking orders accurately, keeping track of what's selling, managing ingredients, and making sure every table gets the right meal at the right time.

Phase 1: Getting Orders Right, Every Time
The first step was giving customers a simple, clean way to browse the menu and place orders from their table. Instead of a server running back and forth to take down orders by hand, customers can view the full menu on a screen, check item ratings, and submit their order directly — all tied to their specific table.

Phase 2: Helping the Team Work Smarter< /strong>
Once orders start coming in, the system goes to work for the staff. The manager can see daily revenue, total orders placed, and which items are performing well — all from one dashboard. Phase 3: Built to Grow
As the restaurant grows, the system grows with it. Future updates will support multiple branches, give different staff members access only to what they need (a kitchen cook doesn't need to see sales reports, and a manager doesn't need to be in the kitchen system), and even predict busy days using sales data.
